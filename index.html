THE PARK NEEDS US

Cannizaro Park is Wimbledon’s backyard—and it needs all who visit to get involved in its care. The Friends of Cannizaro care for the Park but our work wouldn’t be possible without your help. Your donations, membership, and volunteerism ensure it remains a vital public treasure and thriving habitat.

THE FRIENDS OF CANNIZARO CARE FOR THE PARK

From major restorations to ongoing maintenance, we work with Merton Council and your help to keep your park looking amazing.
